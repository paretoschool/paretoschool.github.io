Our Case Studies

Learn From Professionals, Not Academics

We are strong believers in case-based learning. Below are some examples of our 20+ case studies that occur throughout the Pareto MBA program:

First Camp

First Camp is the leading Nordic campsite chain, a so-called "buy'n'build" operation that aims to expand aggressively by buying camp sites and achieve synergies. The company has made over 40 campsite acquisitions during the last few years. We look at:

  • The acquisition process, negotiation tactics and synergies realized.
  • Managing growth and building a management team
  • Metrics and KPIs, including the CEO dashboards and reports

Reason Studios

Reason Studios sells Reason, a music making software used by artists such as The Weekend and Tyler the Creator. tell the story about how the company switched business models from perpetual license to Software as a Service / SaaS. The case study includes:

  • How Reason Studios positions itself in order to compete with industry giants
  • How to calculate the cash flow implications of a switch to SaaS
  • How to manage and organize for change

Netlight Consulting

Netlight Consulting is an IT consulting firm - probably the most successful one in the Nordics given their astounding growth and profitability. Interestingly, a lot of their success can be attributed to a culture that was crafted more than 20 years ago. We look at:

  • The economics of well-run consulting firms
  • How Netlight turned around from near-bankrupcy in 2001
  • The foundations and tactics of the successful Netlight culture
